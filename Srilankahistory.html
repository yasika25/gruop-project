Civil History

Sri Lanka's civil history includes ancient kingdoms, colonial periods, and modern governance structures.


The Sri Lankan Civil War was a conflict between the Sri Lankan government and the LTTE (Liberation Tigers of Tamil Eelam), who demanded a separate Tamil state. Start: 1983 | End: 2009

Reason: Ethnic tensions between Sinhalese and Tamils.

Goal of LTTE: Create an independent Tamil homeland (Tamil Eelam). Result: War ended in 2009 after government forces defeated the LTTE. Thousands died, and the country still works toward peace and unity.

Cultural Heritage

Rich traditions in dance, art, language, and religion shape Sri Lanka's cultural identity.
