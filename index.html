Camping in Sri Lanka

Peaceful nights under the stars in the island’s coolest locations.

Best Camping Locations

  • Horton Plains – misty landscapes
  • Ohiya – calm forests & cold weather
  • Riverston – windy viewpoints
  • Belihuloya – lakeside nature camps

What to Bring

  • Tent + sleeping bag
  • Warm clothes
  • Torch / headlamp
  • Food & water
  • First aid kit

Safety Tips

  • Check weather before camping
  • Do not camp alone
  • Keep food sealed from animals
  • Be careful with campfires

Best Time to Camp

December to April — cool, clear, and perfect for outdoor stays.
